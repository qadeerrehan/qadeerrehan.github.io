Kohli rested for Asia Cup 2018
On 1st September BCCI has announced a 16-member squad for the upcoming Asia Cup. Virat Kohli is given a rest due to his work load and fitness concerns
As usual the right handed opening batsman Rohit Sharma has been named as the captain for the tournament. Ambati Rayudu comes in to the squad after having a failure in yo-yo test ahead of England tour.
Manish Pandey also gets a place in the squad after a tremendous form in recently concluded quadrangular series that featured India A, India B, South Africa A and Australia A. Pandey was unbeaten throughout that tournament.

Suresh Raina who made a come back in England series has been left out after back to back failures. Mayank Agarwal who was expected to get a place, is still had to wait for his chance.

In the bowling line up Umesh Yadav and Siddharth Kaul have been left out. Jasprit Bumrah comes back in to the squad after his finger injury during Ireland tour.

So that the team will have their regular pacers Bhuvneshwar Kumar and Jasprit Bumrah available. Khaleel Ahmed, the 20-year-old left-arm pacer from Rajasthan has earned his maiden India call-up.

India will start their campaign against the team that will get qualified from the on going Asia Cup qualifiers. On the very next day they will take on their arch rivals Pakistan.

 India Squad for Asia Cup 2018 :
Rohit Sharma (c), Shikhar Dhawan (vc), KL Rahul, Ambati Rayudu, Manish Pandey, Kedar Jadhav, MS Dhoni (wk), Dinesh Karthik, Hardik Pandya, Kuldeep Yadav, Yuzvendra Chahal, Axar Patel, Bhuvneshwar Kumar, Jasprit Bumrah, Shardul Thakur, Khaleel Ahmed
